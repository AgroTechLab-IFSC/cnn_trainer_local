CNN Trainer (Local) documentation
=================================

CNN Trainer (Local) is a tool for training Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) on your local machine. It is designed to be easy to use 
and to provide a simple interface for training CNNs on your own data. It supports single-core and multi-core architectures.
